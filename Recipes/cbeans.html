Crocs Beans (CB)

A wonderful British delicacy, served on crocs


Number of Servings: 1
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 canned beans
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ cup white onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp garlic, minced
  • fresh basil, for garnish
  • 1 crocs for plating

Instructions

  1. In a hot pan, drizzle olive oil
  2. Add finely chopped ½ cup white onion. Saute until tender
  3. Once the onion has crystalised, add garlic and tomato paste
  4. Add your can beans and cook until well done
  5. Pour hot beans carefully on your favourite crocs
  6. Add fresh basil for garnish
